The Centre for Inclusive Social Development (CISD India) is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to building an inclusive, equitable, and sustainable society through skill development, employability enhancement, research, healthcare initiatives, environmental protection, and community empowerment.

At CISD, we believe that every individual deserves access to opportunities that enable them to lead a dignified and productive life. Through innovative programmes, partnerships, capacity-building initiatives, and evidence-based interventions, we work towards creating lasting social impact across India.

Our Vision

To create an inclusive, equitable, and sustainable society where every individual has access to opportunities, resources, knowledge, and support to achieve their fullest potential and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Mission

  • Empower youth through industry-relevant skills and employment opportunities.
  • Promote inclusive social and economic development.
  • Strengthen communities through education, health, and livelihood interventions.
  • Conduct high-quality research and impact assessments to support evidence-based decision-making.
  • Protect the environment through sustainable development initiatives.
  • Foster partnerships with government, corporations, educational institutions, and communities.
