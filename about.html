About Us

Who We Are

The Centre for Inclusive Social Development (CISD India) is a multidisciplinary social development organization committed to empowering individuals and strengthening communities through education, skill development, research, healthcare, environmental sustainability, and social innovation.

Established with the vision of creating inclusive growth opportunities, CISD collaborates with educational institutions, government agencies, corporate organizations, non-profits, and community stakeholders to implement impactful development programmes.

Our Journey

CISD Started in year 2015.

Since its inception, CISD has worked towards creating opportunities for youth, supporting communities, conducting research, and strengthening institutions through capacity-building initiatives.

Our work spans multiple sectors and focuses on addressing real-world challenges through practical, sustainable, and scalable solutions.
