Healthcare & Wellbeing

Building Healthier Communities through awareness, education, and service.

Improving Public Health

CISD works to improve public health and community wellbeing. We focus on preventative care, mental health support, and maternal health to ensure holistic development for marginalized populations.

Programme Areas

  • Community Health: Health Awareness Campaigns and Disease Prevention.
  • Mental Health: Stress Management, Counselling, and destigmatizing mental health issues.
  • Women & Child Health: Maternal Health, Adolescent Health, and Nutrition.
  • Community Outreach: Health Screening Camps and Public Health Workshops.
