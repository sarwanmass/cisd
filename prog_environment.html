Environmental Protection

Protecting Nature for Future Generations through community participation.

Our Commitment

CISD promotes environmental sustainability through awareness, action, and active community participation. We believe that sustainable development is impossible without protecting our natural ecosystems.

Key Initiatives

  • Tree Plantation Campaigns: Organizing mass plantation drives in rural and urban areas.
  • Plastic-Free Community Drives: Educating locals on reducing single-use plastics.
  • Water Conservation Programmes: Promoting rainwater harvesting and efficient water usage.
  • Green School Initiatives: Involving students in maintaining school gardens and eco-clubs.
